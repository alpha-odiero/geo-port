# GeoPort Logistics

A modern logistics and transportation company website built using **HTML5**, **CSS3**, and **JavaScript**, featuring a professional corporate design inspired by real-world logistics platforms.

---

## Overview

GeoPort Logistics is a responsive logistics and supply chain management website designed to showcase transportation services, shipment tracking, warehousing solutions, freight management, and logistics operations.

The project was developed as a frontend-focused logistics platform and is structured to support future backend integration using FastAPI and PostgreSQL.

## Pages

### Home

Provides an overview of company services, statistics, testimonials, projects, and logistics solutions.

### About

Contains company history, mission, vision, values, leadership team, and achievements.

### Services

Displays all logistics services offered including:

* Road Freight
* Sea Freight
* Air Freight
* Rail Freight
* Warehousing
* Customs Clearance
* Distribution Services

### Fleet

Showcases transportation assets including trucks, cargo vans, ships, and aircraft.

### Warehousing

Presents storage facilities, inventory management systems, and distribution centers.

### Tracking

Frontend shipment tracking module.

Example Tracking Number:

```text
GP123456
```

### Projects

Highlights completed logistics and transportation projects.

### Gallery

Displays logistics-related images categorized by service type.

### Careers

Lists available positions and allows job application submissions.

### Blog

Shares logistics industry insights and company updates.

### FAQ

Provides answers to frequently asked customer questions.

### Contact

Contains company contact information and inquiry forms.
