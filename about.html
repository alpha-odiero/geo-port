About GeoPort

Our Journey

The GeoPort Logistics Narrative

Established in 2011 with just two light cargo trucks, GeoPort Logistics has evolved into a global powerhouse in the logistical handling and transportation sphere. Our mission remains simple: to eliminate supply chain barriers and establish safe, highly optimized global trade routes for organizations of all sizes.

Over the years, our operations have expanded across air terminal shipping, high-security temperature storage networks, and custom border clearance frameworks, ensuring maximum reliability under all operating constraints.

Guiding Core Values

Mission, Vision & Pillars

Our daily dispatch processes and organizational strategies are grounded in three fundamental operational benchmarks:

Our Mission

To deliver flawless intercontinental shipping logistics powered by continuous tracking updates, fast schedules, and reasonable pricing structures.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the premier, highly-trusted green transportation network that preserves environmental integrity while expanding commerce.

Our Values

Transparency, precision planning, safety certification, and complete professional responsiveness for all shippers and logistics partners.

Our Growth Timeline

Company History

A fast overview of our key accomplishments and milestones over structural development years.

2011

Foundation Days

Started as a local road haulage outfit with only 2 standard freight carriers.

2016

National Expansion

Expanded to a nation-wide carrier fleet of 50+ trucks and introduced basic warehousing space.

2021

Sea and Air Clearance

Established dedicated maritime clearing and cargo air flight handling terminal alliances.

2026

Smart Tech Era

Launched advanced, continuous satellite tracking systems and high-density secure thermal depots.
